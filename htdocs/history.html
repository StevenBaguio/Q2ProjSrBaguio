Origin

The earliest versions of the game have been played since the 7th century. The modern version of the game was very popular since the 13th century. Written works about chess theory began in the 15th century.




Romantic Era

During the 18th century, chess had a resurgence backed by the also popular intellectual movement of romanticism. Players began playing beautifully, rather than objective soundness and strategic planning, players went for beautifully executed tactics and strategies.




Technological Advancements

With the rise of computers, came a new resurgence of chess. The internet enabled a new way to play chess, online chess servers helps us meet new people and a lot of information to help us improve. Nowadays computers have changed the way the sport is played altogether, the newest computers are so powerful that they changed the way we play chess, it introduced better strategies on how to approach a position.
